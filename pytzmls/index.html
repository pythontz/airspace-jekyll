April, 2018

Registration Schedule

About PytzMLSS2018

The PytzMLSS2018 will bring together masters and PhD students and with aim to equip them with necessary cutting-edge and hands-on skills of machine learning and AI research. The school school will provide an introduction to the themes and methods of the field through tutorial and hands-on labs activities. Specifically the tutorial we will cover the foundations of machine learning and deep learning, unsupervised machine learning, reinforcement learning and the application of these techniques in health, natural language processing, nilm and time-series analysis. The emphasis of the tutorial will be on helping participants to understand the underlying motivation for state of the arts machine learning and AI models and how and why they work. The lab activities will equip participants on how to apply these methods to a real world problems in their research and/or in the business career. Lab activities will be based on hands-on training in code development and testing, with application to real data-sets.

Target Audience

In principle, graduate students, doctoral students and researchers in the fields of machine learning and related fields will be typical profiles of participants. However, there are no formal pre-requisites for attendance in terms of academic degrees. Computer scientists who have interests in machine learning and AI and Industry practitioners who desire a more in depth understanding of these subjects are also encouraged to participate.

Requirements and Prerequisites

No deep previous knowledge of ML is required, but the attendants are assumed to have at least some basic background on linear algebra, probabilities and programming in python. A review on basic concepts and an introduction to the necessary tools for implementation exercises will be given in day zero. There is no registration fee for students, non-students participants will pay a registration fees of Tsh 200,000.
